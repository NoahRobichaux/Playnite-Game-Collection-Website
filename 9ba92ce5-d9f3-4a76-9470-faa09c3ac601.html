Description

The people of Hyrule are being swallowed up by strange rifts, with Link and the King of Hyrule among the missing. Left alone, it’s up to Princess Zelda to save her kingdom in a brand-new adventure in the Legend of Zelda™ series!

Team up with an ethereal creature named Tri to use the Tri Rod—a mysterious staff that allows Zelda to create “echoes,” which are imitations of things you find in the environment like objects or monsters. Once you learn an echo, you can use Tri’s power to recreate it whenever you like—no crafting necessary!—to solve puzzles and defeat enemies your own way. To reach a treasure chest, you might stack beds to create a staircase, pick up a crow to glide across a gap, or stack water blocks to swim up to it. The approach is up to you!

Traverse the land’s obstacles and conquer its rifts

Your quest to save Hyrule will span far and wide across its many regions—from the sandstorms of Gerudo Desert to the sparkling lakes and rivers of Jabul Waters. In your travels, you’ll encounter rifts that are spreading across the land. To close the rifts and save the people trapped inside, Zelda and Tri must enter each one and use their powers to traverse broken terrain and defeat dungeon bosses.

Zelda can use other abilities in combination with echoes, including transforming into a powerful swordfighter for a short time. With these abilities, you’ll be fully equipped to tackle every challenge in creative ways.

You can also make travel across Hyrule faster by using waypoints and your horse!

Discover side quests and minigames

As you explore Hyrule, you’ll meet all kinds of interesting people with their own storylines—some dealing with problems great or small, and others who might just have fun challenges for you! You’ll also encounter the engineer Dampé who is looking for someone to help him create powerful wind-up gizmos.

Equip accessories and visit Smoothie Shops for bonus effects

Try on different outfits and accessories to gain various effects such as the ability to jump higher. Visit a Smoothie Shop to blend up your ingredients into tasty drinks capable of replenishing your hearts and granting temporary bonuses like resistance to electricity.

Scan any Legend of Zelda™ themed amiibo™ figures* to get rewards, like smoothie ingredients and certain outfits in alternate colors.
